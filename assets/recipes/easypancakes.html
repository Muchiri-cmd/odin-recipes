Pancakes Recipe

Easy pancakes are a simple and delicious breakfast treat. They consist of a basic pancake batter cooked until golden brown.

To make easy pancakes, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, milk, and eggs in a bowl. Pour ladlefuls onto a hot griddle and flip when bubbles form. Serve with your favorite toppings, like maple syrup or fresh fruit

You can spice up these pancakes by adding chocolate chips, blueberries, or cinnamon to the batter for extra flavor. Easy pancakes are a quick and satisfying way to start your day.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients
  2. Add the wet ingredients and mix
  3. Pou or ladle the batter onto the oiled griddle or pan
  4. Cook until bubbles form,flip and cook other side

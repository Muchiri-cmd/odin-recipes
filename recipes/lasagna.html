Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a layered Italian pasta dish that combines pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel and cheese. It's a hearty, savory delight.

Boil lasagna noodles, prepare a tasty tomato sauce, and a smooth béchamel. Layer noodles with sauce, béchamel, and cheese, and bake until golden and bubbling.

For a veggie option, swap the meat for sautéed mushrooms and spinach. Customize the cheese or add roasted veggies for a comforting lasagna variation

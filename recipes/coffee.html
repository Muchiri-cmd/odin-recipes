Coffee is a popular and aromatic beverage made from roasted coffee beans. It's known for its rich, bold flavor and energizing qualities.

To brew a cup of coffee, grind fresh coffee beans to your preferred coarseness, then add hot water. Allow it to steep briefly, and your coffee is ready to enjoy. You can spice your coffee with sugar, cream, or flavored syrups.

Prep Time

15 min

Cook Time

5 min

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons warm water
  • 2 teaspoons coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup ice,or as needed
  • 6 fluid ounces cold milk

Steps

  1. Combine warm water, instant coffee, and sugar in a sealable jar. Seal and shake until foamy.
  2. Pour into a glass full of ice; add milk. Adjust to taste if necessary.
